What is The Internet Archival Project?

Good question! The Internet Archival Project was the answer to the need of a way to save sites and media on the internet. The Internet Archival Project offers a public use internet archive and repository of websites and media. The archives are available publicly, and updated based on Google's SEO algorithms, meaning websites and media that are drawing wide spread attention are then archived and stored for others to use or view. Archives are available on the left hand side of the website in the navigation menu.



Note:

As of November 7th 2012, The Internet Archival Project will no longer be updating archives or repositories due to a lawsuit involving illegal distribution of copyrighted material. Majority of previous archives will no longer be made available.
